Why BitClip?

Ever wanted to get into bitcoin but couldn't?
Tired of clunky and unhelpful bitcoin services?

BitClip makes bitcoin simple. Gone are the days of having to manage and understand private keys. With a powerfully simple set of features Bitclip takes care of the hastle for you.

Features

  • Encrypted storage of key-address pairs
  • All private keys managed client-side
  • Test Network support
  • In-app market analysis tool
